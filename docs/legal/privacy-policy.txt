FITNESSGURU PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION
Welcome to FitnessGuru ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data when you use our mobile application.

By using FitnessGuru, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our app.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Information You Provide
We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our app:
- Account Information: Your name, email address, and phone number when you create an account
- Profile Information: Optional profile details you choose to add
- Business Information: Your coaching business details and preferences

2.2 Client and Business Data
As a fitness coach, you will input and manage:
- Client Information: Names, contact details, membership types, payment status
- Session Data: Training session schedules, dates, times, locations, and session notes
- Attendance Records: Information about who attended which sessions
- Payment Information: Payment dates, amounts, and payment status (we do NOT store credit card numbers)
- Progress Notes: Training notes, client goals, achievements, and progress tracking

2.3 Automatically Collected Information
- Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent in app
- Error Logs: Technical information if the app crashes or encounters errors
- Location Data: Only if you enable location services for session tracking (optional)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 To Provide and Maintain the Service
- Create and manage your account
- Store and sync your client database across devices
- Enable session scheduling and attendance tracking
- Process and display payment information
- Generate reports and analytics about your business
- Provide customer support

3.2 To Improve Our Service
- Understand how users interact with the app
- Identify and fix bugs
- Develop new features
- Analyze usage patterns (anonymized data)

3.3 To Communicate With You
- Send important service updates and security alerts
- Respond to your support requests
- Send subscription renewal notifications
- Send marketing communications (only with your consent, easy to opt-out)

3.4 For Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Respond to legal requests
- Protect our rights and prevent fraud

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored
Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers located in the European Union. Supabase is a trusted, GDPR-compliant database provider that specializes in secure data storage.

4.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Encryption at Rest: Data stored on servers is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization controls
- Regular Backups: Automated backups to prevent data loss
- Security Audits: Regular security reviews and updates
- Account Security: Strong password requirements and optional two-factor authentication

4.3 Data Retention
- Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- Backups: Backup copies are removed within 90 days
- Legal Requirements: Some data (like payment records) may be kept longer to comply with tax and accounting laws (typically 7 years)

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or client data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Service Providers We Work With
We may share your data with trusted service providers who help us operate the app:
- Supabase: Database hosting and infrastructure (EU servers)
- Stripe: Payment processing for subscriptions (they receive only payment information, not client data)
- Cloud Services: For secure data storage and backups

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law:
- To comply with legal obligations or court orders
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To prevent fraud or security threats
- In connection with an investigation by law enforcement

5.4 Business Transfers
If FitnessGuru is acquired by or merged with another company, your data may be transferred to the new owners. We will notify you before this happens.

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

6.1 Access Your Data
- You can view all your data within the app at any time
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format (CSV/JSON)
- Contact us to request a complete data export

6.2 Correct Your Data
- Update your account information in the app settings
- Edit or correct any client or session data
- Contact us if you need help correcting information

6.3 Delete Your Data
- Delete individual clients, sessions, or notes at any time
- Delete your entire account and all associated data from Settings → Account → Delete Account
- Request data deletion by emailing us (see contact section below)

6.4 Export Your Data
- Export client lists, session records, and payment history
- Download data in standard formats (CSV, PDF)
- Take your data to another service if you wish

6.5 Opt-Out of Marketing
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any email
- Disable push notifications in app settings
- Contact us to be removed from all marketing communications

6.6 GDPR Rights (For EU Users)
If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:
- Right to Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
- Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
- Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
- Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing
- Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time
- Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
FitnessGuru is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

8. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS
Your data is stored on servers located in the European Union (Supabase). If you are located outside the EU, your data will be transferred to and stored in the EU. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

9. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES
FitnessGuru uses minimal tracking technologies:
- Essential Cookies: Required for app functionality (authentication, preferences)
- Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (optional, can be disabled)
- No Advertising Trackers: We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels

You can manage your data collection preferences in the app settings.

10. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION
In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:
- Notify you within 72 hours of discovering the breach
- Explain what happened and what data was affected
- Describe the steps we're taking to address the issue
- Provide recommendations to protect your information
- Report the breach to relevant authorities as required by law

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes:
- We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- We will notify you via email or app notification for significant changes
- Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
- We will never reduce your rights without your explicit consent
